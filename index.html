Here's the recipe

  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 cups (360g) honey
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (120g) all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup (100g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Optional: 1 cup (175g) chocolate chips or chopped nuts for added texture

Instruction

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted butter and honey. Mix until well combined.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  7. If desired, fold in the chocolate chips or chopped nuts.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it out evenly.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs. Be careful not to overbake.
  10. Remove from the oven and let the brownies cool completely in the pan before cutting into squares.
